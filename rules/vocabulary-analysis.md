# Vocabulary Analysis

Extract these patterns from the scraped website content.

## 1. Signature Phrases

Find 5-8 phrases that are distinctive to this brand. Not generic ("we help businesses") but specific to how THEY talk.

Look for:
- Phrases that appear on multiple pages (repeated = intentional)
- Unusual word combinations
- Branded terms or coined phrases
- Consistent metaphors or frameworks they use

## 2. Power Words

Words they lean on heavily. These define their vocabulary fingerprint.

Categories:
- **Action verbs** they use most (build, scale, transform, deploy, etc.)
- **Adjectives** they rely on (proven, strategic, comprehensive, etc.)
- **Industry terms** they use naturally (not forced jargon)

## 3. Avoided Words (Inferred)

Words that are conspicuously absent. This is often more revealing than what they use.

Check for absence of:
- Hype words (revolutionary, game-changing, disruptive) — absence = disciplined
- Hedging words (might, maybe, hopefully) — absence = confident
- Filler phrases (in order to, at the end of the day, leverage) — absence = tight writing
- Competitor-typical words — what does their industry usually say that they don't?

## 4. Sentence Patterns

- **Average sentence length** (count words in 20 representative sentences)
- **Paragraph length** (average sentences per paragraph)
- **Use of fragments** (intentional incomplete sentences)
- **Question usage** (do they ask questions? Rhetorical or direct?)
- **List usage** (bullet points, numbered lists, or prose-only?)

## 5. Proof Style

How do they substantiate claims?

| Style | Example |
|-------|---------|
| Numbers | "290+ compositions," "6-day cycle → 4 hours" |
| Stories | "One client came to us after..." |
| Mechanisms | "The system works by extracting..." |
| Social | "Trusted by 500+ companies" |
| None | Claims with no adjacent proof |

## Output Format

```
VOCABULARY FINGERPRINT
════════════════════════════════════

Signature Phrases:
  • "[phrase 1]"
  • "[phrase 2]"
  • "[phrase 3]"
  • "[phrase 4]"
  • "[phrase 5]"

Power Words: [word], [word], [word], [word], [word]

Notably Absent: [word], [word], [word]

Avg Sentence Length: [X] words
Paragraph Style: [short/medium/long]
Proof Style: [primary style]

════════════════════════════════════
```
